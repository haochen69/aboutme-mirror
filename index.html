Why Scholara

Built for the whole
learning village

Great education doesn't happen in isolation. Scholara brings together every person in a student's world — their teachers, parents, and administrators — with the tools to make learning stick.

📐

Standards-Aligned Resources

Every lesson, worksheet, and activity in Scholara maps directly to Common Core, NGSS, and state-level academic standards. You'll always know exactly what skills are being addressed and why — so planning periods become planning victories.

📊

Teacher Dashboards That Work

See your whole class at a glance: who's on track, who needs a nudge, and what topics deserve more time. Scholara's teacher dashboard turns data into clear, actionable next steps rather than a spreadsheet you'll never open again.

📈

Student Progress Tracking

Students see their own growth through visual progress maps that celebrate milestones and make goals feel reachable. Learning becomes a conversation, not a verdict, with weekly check-ins and skill mastery badges built right in.

👨‍👩‍👧

Parent Visibility Tools

Parents receive a weekly learning summary in plain language — no app download required. Know what your child is working on, celebrate wins together, and reach out to their teacher with one tap when you want to connect.

Getting Started

Up and running in
under ten minutes

No professional development day required. Teachers, parents, and students all get where they're going fast.

1

Create Your Free Account

Sign up with your school email in 60 seconds. Teachers get a classroom code; parents enter their child's school and join the learning network. No credit card, ever, for the free tier.

2

Find & Assign Resources

Filter by grade level, subject, standard code, or time available to find the right lesson immediately. Assign to your class, a small group, or individual students — and differentiate with one click.

3

Track Growth Together

Watch students progress on live dashboards. Parents receive a plain-language weekly digest. Administrators see school-wide trends. Everyone stays in the loop — no extra meetings needed.
